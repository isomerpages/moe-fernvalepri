<h3><strong>Video Category</strong></h3>
<p><strong>Title: My Journey... A Full Circle</strong>
<br>
<br>Tham Ka Yan&nbsp;</p>
<p>Ridwan Bin Razali</p>
<p>Chen Hongzhi Jareth
<br>
<br>
<br>A personal story of growth and returning to where it all started.</p>
<p>Some stories begin with a simple hello. In a primary school where hundreds
of voices fill the air, one boy's silence speaks the loudest. At seven,
his world is a bubble of solitude until a classmate's smile cracks its
surface. One friendship leads to many, and slowly, the walls of isolation
crumble. Six years shape him from a boy who hides to one who thrives.</p>
<p>Years later, he chooses to return, carrying memories of both loneliness
and triumph. Now a teacher, he recognizes the familiar quiet of uncertainty
in his students' eyes. Each day becomes an opportunity to extend the same
hand that once helped him find his way.</p>
